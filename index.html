About Us

Galaxy Auto Service is an independent car workshop based in Berlin, founded by Alimar Aljabi – a certified automotive mechatronics technician.

Known for its honest and efficient approach, Galaxy Auto Service delivers high-Standard care — treating every vehicle with the same attention to detail we’d expect for our own.

Diagnostics & Repairs

  • Vehicle Diagnostics and Error Analysis
  • Engine Repair and Servicing
  • Mechanical Repairs (Engine, Exhaust, Suspension)
  • Electrical and Electronics Repair
  • Brake Inspection and Replacement

Maintenance Services

  • Routine Maintenance and Seasonal Inspections
  • Oil Change Services
  • Battery Testing and Replacement
  • Tire and Wheel Services (Mounting, Balancing, Repair)

Technology & Upgrades
